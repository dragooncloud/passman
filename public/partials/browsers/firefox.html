Firefox

Firefox already has a password manager built into it, but doesn't synchronise with your other browsers or devices; therefore in order to prevent both from running at the same time, we need to switch it off.

  1. Click the button in the top right corner of your browser and choose Options.
  2. On the options page click Privacy & Security on the left and untick the first option Always save logins and passwords for websites.
  3. All done! You can now close the Options page.
